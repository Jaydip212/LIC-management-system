Who We Are

India's Premier Digital Insurance Management Platform

Founded in 2000, the LIC Management System was established with a singular mission: to make life insurance accessible, transparent, and hassle-free for every Indian family.

Today, with over 10 lakh policyholders, 500+ certified agents across 28 states, and a claim settlement ratio of 98.6%, we are one of the most trusted insurance management platforms in the country.

Our digital-first approach combined with our pan-India ground network means you get the best of both worlds — the convenience of technology and the warmth of personal service.
