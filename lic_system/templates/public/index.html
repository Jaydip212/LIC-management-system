LIC offers a free look period of 15 days (30 days for online/distance policies) from the date of receipt of the plan. During this period, you can return the policy if you are not satisfied with its terms and conditions and receive a full refund of premium paid, minus any applicable charges.

Filing a claim is simple: Contact your assigned agent or call our helpline 1800-222-4567. Submit required documents (death certificate / medical bills / accident report). Our team verifies and processes the claim within 7 working days. Payment is credited directly to the nominee's bank account.

If you miss a premium payment, your policy enters a grace period (30 days for monthly, 30-60 days for other modes). If payment is not made within the grace period, the policy lapses. You can revive a lapsed policy within 5 years by paying all overdue premiums with interest and submitting a health declaration.
